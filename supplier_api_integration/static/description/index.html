Mixed Inventory

Warehouse stock +
supplier stock = full clarity.

When customers see both your in-house stock and your supplier's remote stock — with proper delivery messages — conversion goes up. Four configurable scenarios cover every selling pattern.

WAREHOUSE 0 SUPPLIER 7 CONTINUE SELLING OFF
Customer sees "7 units in remote stock · Ship 4-8 days". No back-orders accepted beyond what the supplier has.
WAREHOUSE 0 SUPPLIER 7 CONTINUE SELLING ON
Customer ordered 10. Site shows 7 from supplier (4-8 days) + 3 on back-order (3 weeks).
WAREHOUSE 4 SUPPLIER 7 CONTINUE SELLING OFF
Two-tier display: 4 fast (1-2 days) + 7 slower (4-8 days). Customer chooses.
WAREHOUSE 4 SUPPLIER 7 CONTINUE SELLING ON
Customer ordered 15. Site lays out 4 + 7 + 4 back-order. Maximum revenue capture.
